Get Started

Oreo Cupcakes Recipe

To make Oreo cupcakes, preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake tin with paper liners. In a bowl, combine 1 1/2 cups of flour, 1/2 cup of cocoa powder, 1 teaspoon of baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda, and a pinch of salt. In another bowl, beat together 1 cup of sugar, 1/2 cup of softened butter, 2 eggs, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract until creamy. Gradually add the dry ingredients, alternating with 1 cup of milk, until the batter is smooth. Fold in crushed Oreo cookies. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let cool completely before frosting with your favorite Oreo-infused buttercream and topping with additional crushed Oreos.

Blue Berry Smothie

To make a refreshing blueberry smoothie, blend together 1 cup of fresh or frozen blueberries, 1 banana, 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup of milk (or a milk alternative), 1 tablespoon of honey or maple syrup, and a handful of ice cubes. Blend until smooth and creamy. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately. For added nutrition, you can also add a handful of spinach or a tablespoon of chia seeds.

Chocolate Shake Recipe

In a blender, combine 2 cups of cold milk, 3 scoops of chocolate ice cream, 2 tablespoons of chocolate syrup, and 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract. Blend until smooth and creamy. Pour into a glass, top with whipped cream if desired, and enjoy! For extra richness, add a tablespoon of peanut butter or a banana before blending. Use cold milk for a frostier shake.

Pancakes Recipe

To make delicious pancakes, whisk together 1 cup of flour, 2 tablespoons of sugar, 1 tablespoon of baking powder, and a pinch of salt. In another bowl, combine 1 cup of milk, 1 egg, and 2 tablespoons of melted butter. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.

Chocolate Frosting Recipe

To make a rich chocolate frosting, beat 1 cup of softened butter until creamy. Gradually add 3 1/2 cups of powdered sugar, 1/2 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder, 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Mix in 1/4 cup of heavy cream or milk, adding more if needed, until the frosting is smooth and spreadable. Beat on high for an additional 2-3 minutes for a light and fluffy texture. Spread over your favorite cakes or cupcakes and enjoy!
